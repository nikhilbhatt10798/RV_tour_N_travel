Vasuki Tal

The trek to Vasuki Tal is generally completed in the day itself from Kedarnath, with trekkers returning to Kedarnath to spend the night. Some adventurous trekkers can start trekking right away from, while others can take a pony ride till Kedarnath shrine and then trek towards Vasuki Tal. It is 8km trek from Kedarnath and 24 kms trek from nearest road head at Gauri Kund.
